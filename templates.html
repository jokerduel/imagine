Details

These are the original Awakening card templates. Use Parchment MF for the title font and Ariel MT for the text boxes. High resolution Bograth, Paradwyn, d'Resh, and Nar templates are not available as originals.

Custom card templates were created by starting with an upscaled card, removing the text with Flux Kontext, manually cropping out the art, adding back the drop shadow, and in some cases restoring the title box.

Open the image before copying to clipboard to use the full resolution PNG instead of the thumbnail preview.
